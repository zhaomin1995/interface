1. There would be one/twenty-one tweet(s) you need to read. Read the tweet(s) carefully.

2. If there are more than one tweet you need to read, take the middle one as the anchor tweet and the remaining as the context tweets, then for the anchor tweet, answer the two questions below:

  1. If the author of the tweet was in the location (highlighted in red) when he/she was posting.

Answer Choices:

For question 1, the answer could be Yes, No, or Invalid.

  • Yes means that the author of the tweet was in the place when posting.
  • No means that the author of the tweet was not in the place when posting.
  • Invalid means that location is not a real 'location'. For example, 'Charlotte' is the name of a person rather than the name of the city.

For question 2, what you should do is to choose a confidence level for answering the question 1. You need to choose the number that represents your confidence level from 1 to 5 (1 represents the lowest confidence while 5 represents the highest confidence).
