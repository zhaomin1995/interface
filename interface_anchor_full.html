Instructions

You will see a tweet and your job is to determine whether the author of the tweet was located in some city when the tweet was published. The city will always be named in the tweet. Specifically, you will answer two questions:

1. Was the author of the tweet located in the city when the tweet was published?

  • Yes, the author of the tweet was located in the city when the tweet was published.
  • No, the author of the tweet was not located in the city when the tweet was published.
  • Invalid, the city is not a location in the context of this tweet.

2. How confident are you about your answer?

  • Extremely confident
  • Very confident
  • Moderately confident
  • Slightly confident
  • Not confident at all

Examples

The right label is Yes because the author of the tweet was attending a picnic in Arlington when she made this tweet.

The right label is No because we can tell that the author of the tweet was NOT at Miami when he/she made this tweet.

The right label is Invalid because we can tell that the Washington is not a 'real' location in the context of this tweet.
