Identify hate speech and counter-hate speech

Instructions:

In this task, you will see a short Reddit comment posted by a User. Your job is to label the comment as Hate, Counter-hate or Neutral.

Definitions and Examples (click HERE to see more details and examples):

[Hate] The comment attacks an individual or a group with the intention to vilify, humiliate, or incite hatred.

[Counter-hate] The comment counters hate speech. Scenario examples:

  1. User presents facts to correct misstatements
  2. User warns offline or online consequences
  3. User appeals someone to calm down
  4. User disagrees with the hate in humor or sarcasm

[Neutral] The comment neither conveys hate nor counters hate speech.
