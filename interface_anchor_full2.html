Instructions

You will see a tweet and your job is to determine how long the author has stayed at some city. The city will always be named in the tweet. Specifically, you will answer one question:

"How long the author of the tweet has stayed at the city?"

You need to determine the units of the maximum period and minimum period that the author of the tweet has stayed at the city. If you think the author of the tweet did not stay at the city, you can click the radio button on the last line of the question.

Tweets & Question

Question

How long has the author stayed at ${anchor_location}?

The author of the tweet could stay at the ${anchor_location} for at least.

The author of the tweet could stay at the ${anchor_location} for at most.

The author was not at the ${anchor_location} when he/she made this tweet.
